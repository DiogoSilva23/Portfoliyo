Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on the platform?

To create an account, simply click on the "Login" button on any page, followed by the "Sign up now!" link that will redirect you to the page you can create an account. You will need to provide the requested information, that will be different depending if you are a Professional User or an Enterprise.

What information is required to create a profile?

If you are a Professional User you will need to provide username, name, email, password, location, description, gender and birthdate. In case you are an Enterprise you will need to provide name, email, password, website url, logo url and description.

How can I update my profile information?

To update your profile, go to your profile page and click on the "Edit Profile" button. From there, you can make changes to your personal information, work experience, education, skills, and any other relevant details. Remember to save your changes once you're done.

How do I connect with other professionals on the platform?

Connecting with other professionals is easy. You can search for specific individuals by name. When you find someone you want to connect with, simply send them a connection request, once they accept your request, you'll be connected.

What are the benefits of having a complete profile?

Having a complete profile increases your visibility and enhances your professional credibility. It allows others to understand your background, skills, and experience, making it easier for potential employers, recruiters, or business partners to find you. A comprehensive profile also helps you showcase your expertise and interests to engage with relevant opportunities and connections.

How can I search for job opportunities on the platform?

To search for job opportunities, you can use the platform's dedicated job search feature. Enter relevant keywords, location, industry, or job title to refine your search.

Can I upload my resume or CV to my profile?

Yes, you can upload your resume or CV to your profile. This allows employers and recruiters to review your complete professional background. Uploading a resume can also help the platform's algorithms suggest relevant job opportunities based on the information provided.

How do I manage my privacy settings?

You can manage your privacy settings by accessing the "Edit Profile" button on your profile page. From there, you can control who can view your profile, contact you, and access your information, by switching the checkbox of your visibility.

What features are available for job seekers?

The platform offers various features to assist job seekers. These may include job search filters and personalized job recommendations, the ability to apply for jobs directly through the platform.

Can I promote my business or services on the platform?

Yes, the platform provides opportunities for business promotion. You can create a company page to showcase your business and engage with a professional audience.
